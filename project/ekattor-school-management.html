Ekattor School Management System

Ekattor School Management System is a complete digital platform for schools, offering admission, attendance, exams, fees, library, hostel, communication, and reporting—all in one secure dashboard.

Key Features

Complete student admission and enrollment management
Automated attendance tracking with SMS notifications
Comprehensive exam management and grade book
Online fee payment and receipt generation
Digital library with book tracking and fines
Hostel management with room allocation
Parent-teacher communication portal
Student performance reports and analytics
Transport management system
Multi-branch support for institutions

Overview

Ekattor School Management System is a complete digital platform designed to streamline all school operations. From student admission to examination management, fee collection to library tracking, the system provides a centralized solution for administrators, teachers, students, and parents. The platform ensures efficient communication and transparent reporting across all stakeholders.
